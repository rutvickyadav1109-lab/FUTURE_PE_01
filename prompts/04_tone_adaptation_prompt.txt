As part of my prompt engineering internship task, 
I am building a reusable website copy framework 
that can work for different types of local businesses.
To demonstrate adaptability, I first wrote a homepage 
headline for my client Maya Garments 
(a clothing store in Boisar, Maharashtra):
"Boisar's Favourite Stop for Styles That Fit Every Life"
This headline was crafted with:
- Tone: Warm, local, community-feel
- Audience: Families and young adults in Boisar
- Core USP: Wide variety + branded clothes + honest prices
Now I want to show how the same value proposition 
framework can be adapted for 3 different 
local business types in the same town:
1. A local hair salon in Boisar
   Target tone: Fun, confidence-boosting, friendly
2. A medical or dental clinic in Boisar
   Target tone: Calm, trustworthy, professional
3. A coaching institute in Boisar
   Target tone: Motivational, ambitious, encouraging
For each adaptation please write:
- A new headline (max 10 words)
- A new sub-headline (1-2 lines)
- 1 line explaining what tone change was made and why
This output will be part of my internship submission 
to show that my prompt framework is reusable 
across multiple industries.
